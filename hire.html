The Web Design Process

Ask Questions

The first phase of any web design project is asking the right questions. The hope is to learn more about you and your company's needs and audience. During this phase, we can discuss an overall theme and vision for the site.

Design Mock-ups

Next, I create a design mock-ups of each of the major pages of your site. You can view the site as I build it and provide feedback. We'll meet regularly for status updates until the design is finalized.

Develop on Wordpress

I will then create a Wordpress version of the website and begin filling in the major content. During this phase, the only people that will be able to access the site is you and me. The public won't be able to view the site yet.

Go Live!

The final stage is deploying the site. After I receive the go ahead from you, I'll go live with the site. But, my services don't end there. I'll be available for 24/7 support and maintenance for as long as you use the site.

Propose a Project!
